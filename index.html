    gameoflife.net

    Conway's game of life in .NET using BDD/TDD and OOP

    • Somewhat modularized, meaning that the view can be separated from the calculation engine.
    • The rules are thoroughly tested via scenario/unit/behaviour tests (SUB-tests? Is that even a term? =))
    • The idea was to initially be free of the grid and just have cells with relations, but that didn't quite pan out
    • Performance is very slow, but the code can be useful as a study example, I guess.

    The starting grid is randomized and R resets the "game", space moves forward an iteration.
